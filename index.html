About Alaba

Alaba is an area of Lagos popularly known for the Alaba International Market. This market is so popular that people from all over Nigeria, even those who have never been to Lagos, know about it.

Alaba International Market is more of a wholesale market, attracting traders and buyers from across the country.

There are other markets in Lagos, but none as popular as Alaba. For example, there's Balogun Market, but you can never compare both.
